Introduction

Antiviral Drug Resource and Design Platform(AVDRD) provides the search, display and design functions of experimental knowledge-based antiviral drugs, include vaccines, antibodies and small molecule compounds. For vaccines and antibodies, name, mechanism of action, clinical stage, and clinical research results are displayed. For potential therapeutic viral targets, target name, sequence, function, protein structure, and pathway are annotated. In addition, the small molecule drug activity information corresponding to each viral therapeutic target is also displayed. Data in AVDRD is annually updated and freely served for biologists and medicinal chemists interested in antiviral drugs.
